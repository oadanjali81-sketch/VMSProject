# QR Scanner - Quick Start Guide

## ✅ How It Works Now

### Simple 3-Step Process:

1. **Click "Start Scanner"** 
   - Browser will ask for camera permission
   - Click "Allow" in the popup

2. **Camera Opens Immediately**
   - Your laptop camera turns on
   - Video feed appears on screen

3. **Show QR Code**
   - Hold visitor's QR code in front of camera
   - Check-in/Check-out happens AUTOMATICALLY
   - No need to click anything!

## 🚀 Usage Instructions

### For Check-In:
1. Visitor arrives with QR code (printed or on phone)
2. Admin clicks "Start Scanner"
3. Camera opens
4. Hold QR code in front of camera (6-12 inches away)
5. **BEEP!** ✓ Check-in happens automatically
6. Visitor details appear on right side
7. Activity log updates

### For Check-Out:
1. Visitor shows same QR code
2. Hold in front of camera
3. **BEEP!** ✓ Check-out happens automatically
4. Duration calculated and displayed

---

## 💡 Tips for Best Results

### Camera Position:
- Keep camera at eye level
- Ensure good lighting (no shadows on QR)
- Avoid glare from overhead lights

### QR Code:
- Hold steady (don't shake)
- Distance: 6-12 inches from camera
- Angle: Face QR directly at camera
- Size: Minimum 2x2 inches works best

### Environment:
- Good lighting is essential
- Avoid backlighting (window behind visitor)
- Clean camera lens if blurry

## 🔧 Troubleshooting

### Camera Won't Open?

**Check 1: Permission**
- Look for camera icon in address bar
- Click it and select "Allow"
- Refresh page

**Check 2: Other Apps**
- Close Zoom, Teams, Skype
- Close other browser tabs using camera
- Restart browser

**Check 3: Hardware**
- Check if camera is connected (external webcam)
- Try built-in laptop camera
- Check Device Manager (Windows)

### QR Not Scanning?

**Try:**
- Move QR closer/farther
- Improve lighting
- Hold QR steady for 1-2 seconds
- Clean camera lens
- Print QR larger

**Alternative:**
- Click "Manual Entry"
- Type pass number (e.g., VIS-ABC123)
- Click "Process"

## ⚡ Performance

- **Scan Speed**: 250ms (4 scans per second)
- **Detection Time**: Usually < 1 second
- **Cooldown**: 2 seconds between same QR

## 🔒 Security & Privacy
